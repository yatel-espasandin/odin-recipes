Ghee Butter Steak

We are going to prepare a ghee butter steak in just 15 minutes

This is a very powerful recipe because it's easy to prepare and have a heavy nutritional density, all you need if you have a lot of work in the day.

Here's what you need:
  • 500 gr of steak, choose the meat you want.
  • 150 gr of ghee butter.
  • 2 sprig of rosemary.
Steps? Follow this:
  1. Take a frying pan and put it in strong fire.
  2. When the pan is very hot, put 100gr of ghee butter and let it melt.
  3. Peel by hand one rosemary sprig and throw it in to the ghee butter.
  4. Take the steak and make it scream with the melted butter for 5 minutes each side.
  5. In another pot put 50gr of ghee butter and keep it in company with the other rosemary sprig.
  6. Pour the rosemary ghee butter mixture over the steak.
  7. No more steps, let's eat
